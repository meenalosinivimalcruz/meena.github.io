About me
Meenalosini Vimal Cruz M.sc.,M.Phil.,M.Tech.,Ph.D

Hi, Welcome to my page, I am Meenalosini Vimal Cruz, working as an assistant professor at Keene State College, Computer Science departement,USNH, USA since 2017. I completed my Ph.D (computer science engineering) in a technical university at 2014. I received my Master of Technology degree in Computer and Information technology with gold medal from a state university in India. I have completed Master of philosophy and Master of Science in Physics. I have published more than 8 papers in reputed international journals and has google index 4. My research focus is in Digital Image processing, Machine learning and Computer aided Diagnosis in medical images. I have been serving as an editorial board member in many reputed international conferences and journals. My area of specialization is Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning and Data science. I have a beautiful family with two lovely kids and lovable husband who are the reason behind my every sucess. My hobbies are painting and Yoga.
